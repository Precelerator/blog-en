Precelerator goes digital: This is what you can expect from the launch of the brand new offer at the HM

By Fabio Maienschein
02/10/2020

With the launch of the new website and this blog, the Precelerator, the new offer of the Strascheg Center for Entrepreneurship at HM digital, starts the new semester. Here you can find out for whom the new Precelerator is intended and which Precelerator offers will be available soon.

The Precelerator is a brand new initiative at the Strascheg Center for Entrepreneurship (SCE) targeted at makers and innovators that don't necessarily aim for founding their own startup, but instead are looking for help and resources to get their project off the ground. Providing both technical staff as well as mentors and coaches through the SCE, the Precelerator is the ideal place to connect with others and to work on innovative ideas and initiatives.

If you want to learn more, keep your eyes out for our digital launch in November, we're excited to share with you what we have been working on over the last months!

Call To Action

Interested in joining the precelerator as a member or collaborator? We're always open for project suggestions, get in touch using our email precelerator@sce.de and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
